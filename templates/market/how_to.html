How to list game accounts in our market place

  1. You need to ensure your account is added onto your account list in your Profile dashboard
  2. Click on Dashboard under Market then on >> Start Account Listing
  3. The a Modal Box will appear allowing you to fill in your account details - this includes your game id, selling price, Login Email (im30), Password and Pin if available
  4. Once you are done your account will start appearing in your dashboard with a status indicator indicating the account is not yet listed
  5. Click your account and then add extra details, like number of gold sets, gold cards, and etch
  6. Update your account and then it will appear in puvlic
